Pork Burritos Recipe

This is a simple recipe for making pork burritos.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 4 large tortillas
  • Shredded cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, cook the ground pork over medium heat until browned.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pork, rice, black beans, and salsa.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Spoon the pork mixture onto each tortilla and sprinkle with cheese if desired.
  5. Roll up the tortillas and serve with extra salsa on the side.
