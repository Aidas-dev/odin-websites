Boiled Buckwheat Recipe

This is a simple recipe for making boiled buckwheat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buckwheat groats
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the buckwheat groats under cold water.
  2. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed buckwheat and a pinch of salt.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve.
